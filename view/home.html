BSED: Brazil Socio-Economic Database



Brazil Socio-Economic Database (BSED) is a data source and analysis platform that supports informatics studies in socio-economy and healthcare. BSED hosts a unique dataset compiled from various data sources containing annual socio-economic and health indicators for the 5560 Brazil municipalities in the 12-year period from 2000 to 2011.
